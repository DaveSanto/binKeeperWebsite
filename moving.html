Packing Tips

8 Tips for a Smoother Move

From professional packers and seasoned movers — plus how BinKeeper makes each one easier.

01

Start with one room at a time

Packing room-by-room reduces chaos. BinKeeper's room folders keep your inventory organized the same way.

02

Heavy items in small boxes

Books and tools go in small boxes — your back will thank you. Log weight notes in BinKeeper so movers handle them right.

03

Pack an "open first" box

Toilet paper, phone charger, coffee. Tag it in BinKeeper as Priority #1 so it's never lost in the stack.

04

Photograph valuables before packing

Electronics, art, jewelry — snap a photo before boxing. BinKeeper stores photos with every item for insurance purposes.

05

Label every side of the box

Or better yet — skip the pen entirely. A BinKeeper QR code on one side tells you everything, no matter which side faces out.

06

Reuse plastic bins for storage

Plastic totes make better long-term storage than cardboard. BinKeeper QR tags stay attached and the inventory never leaves.

07

Don't mix rooms in one box

It feels efficient but creates chaos at the other end. BinKeeper's room tags make this easy to enforce as you pack.

08

Log as you pack, not after

The hardest thing about moving inventory is remembering what's where. BinKeeper in hand while packing = zero guesswork later.
