One scan. Everything inside.

The QR label is BinKeeper's killer feature. Stick one on every bin and you'll never have to lift a lid to know what's inside. Pull up your phone, scan, and the full contents list — with photos — appears instantly. No app navigation required.

Auto-generated

Every bin gets a unique QR code the moment you create it in BinKeeper. No setup, no configuration — it just appears, ready to print.

Print at home

Print labels directly from the app — standard letter size, or any label paper you already own. No special equipment needed.

Instant contents

Scan the QR code and see the bin's name, room, photo, and full items list. No app login required for viewing — just scan and go.

Always up to date

The QR code links live to your bin. Update the bin's contents in the app and the next scan shows the latest — no reprinting required.
