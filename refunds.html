Refund Policy

We want BinKeeper to be worth every penny. If something goes wrong, here's exactly what we'll do about it.

Effective date: April 2026

Short version: subscriptions can be cancelled anytime and you keep access until the period ends. If the service failed to work as described and we can't fix it, we'll refund your most recent charge within 7 days. One-time purchases (BinKeeper Move, item add-ons) are non-refundable once the features have been used. If you're unsure, just email us — we'll work something out.

1. Subscriptions (Keeper and Keeper Pro)

Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. When you cancel:

  • Your subscription will not renew at the end of the current billing period
  • You keep full access to your plan features until the period ends
  • We do not prorate partial months — there are no partial refunds for unused days

Refunds on subscription charges

We will issue a full refund of your most recent subscription charge if:

  • You contact us within 7 days of the charge, and
  • You experienced a material failure — the app was down, a core feature didn't work as described, or we made a billing error

We will not issue refunds for:

  • Simply changing your mind after using the service
  • Forgetting to cancel before a renewal date
  • Requests made more than 7 days after the charge

If you forgot to cancel and were charged for a renewal, email us promptly. We'll review it. We're a small company and we'd rather keep a happy customer than argue over one month.

2. One-time purchases

Purchase Refundable? Conditions
BinKeeper Move ($25) Within 24 hours of purchase if no AI scans have been used Once AI scanning has been used or the 30-day period has begun and more than 24 hours have passed, no refund is available
Item capacity add-on ($0.99/bin) Not refundable after purchase The additional item capacity is applied immediately and permanently to your bin

If you purchased BinKeeper Move and the service failed to function at all — for example, you couldn't log in or the app crashed immediately — contact us and we'll refund you regardless of the above.

3. Billing errors

If you were charged incorrectly — wrong amount, duplicate charge, charge after cancellation — we will always fix it. Contact us at admin@binkeeperapp.com with your account email and a description of the issue. We'll investigate and issue a correction within 5 business days.

We use Stripe for all payment processing. If you believe a charge is fraudulent, you may also contact your bank or card issuer directly.

4. Free plan

BinKeeper's Free plan has no charge. There is nothing to refund. If you're on the Free plan and something isn't working, just contact us and we'll help.

5. How to request a refund

Email us at admin@binkeeperapp.com with:

  • The email address on your BinKeeper account
  • The date and amount of the charge
  • A brief description of the issue

We'll respond within 2 business days. If a refund is approved, it will be processed through Stripe back to your original payment method. Refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

6. Our commitment

We built BinKeeper because we needed it ourselves. We're not trying to trap anyone with confusing terms or make it hard to get money back when something goes wrong. If you have a problem, just talk to us. We'll do right by you.

BinKeeper is operated by Santtro LLC. This policy applies to all purchases made on binkeeperapp.com.
