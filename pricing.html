Common questions

Is the Free plan really free?

Yes — no credit card, no trial timer, no catch. You get 2 bins and up to 10 items per bin, forever. You also get 5 lifetime AI scans so you can try that feature before committing. When you're ready to add more, upgrading takes about 30 seconds.

What happens when I hit my bin limit?

The app will prompt you to upgrade before you can create a new bin. Your existing bins and everything in them stay exactly as they are.

What are the item limits per bin?

Free plan bins hold up to 10 items each. Keeper bumps that to 40. Keeper Pro removes the limit entirely. On Free and Keeper, you can also expand any individual bin by 50 items for $0.99 — stackable, per bin, one-time.

How does AI Item ID work?

Point your camera at an item and the app identifies it automatically — no typing required. Free users get 5 lifetime scans to try the feature. Keeper Pro gets 50 per calendar month, resetting on the 1st. BinKeeper Move includes 150 scans valid for your 30-day window. Your remaining scan count is always visible in the app.

Why is AI not included on Keeper?

AI identification uses real compute to process every photo. Free gets 5 lifetime scans as a taste of the feature. Keeper Pro gets 50/month as a subscription perk. BinKeeper Move includes 150 scans purpose-built for a single move. Keeping AI out of the Keeper tier lets us price it genuinely affordably — that plan is about capacity and sharing, not AI.

What is BinKeeper Move?

BinKeeper Move is a $25 one-time purchase that gives you full Pro-level access for 30 days — unlimited bins, unlimited items, and 150 AI scans. It's designed for moving: catalog every box before the truck arrives, then find anything on the other end. After 30 days your account returns to Free, but all your bins and items are preserved. You can purchase it again for a future move.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time. Annual plans save you 10% on Keeper and 15% on Keeper Pro. If you downgrade, your existing bins and items are preserved in read-only mode until you're within your new plan's limits.

What does household sharing mean?

Keeper and Keeper Pro let you invite anyone in your household. They can view, add, and edit bins and items — one shared system for the whole house.

Can I cancel any time?

Absolutely. Cancel from your account with no penalties. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
