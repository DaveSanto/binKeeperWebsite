# BinKeeper — Features & Product Scope
*Handoff context for app and website development*

---

## Core Concept

BinKeeper is a household and group inventory management app built around physical bins, boxes, and containers. Users label their bins with QR codes, photograph and catalog contents, and find items instantly by searching. The app serves both private household use and shared group scenarios where physical stuff lives somewhere and multiple people need visibility into it.

---

## Feature List

### 1. QR Code Bin Labeling
- Each bin gets a unique QR code generated by the app
- User prints or affixes the QR code to the physical bin
- Scanning the QR code in the app opens that bin instantly
- Core differentiator — turns any physical container into a searchable inventory

### 2. Item Entry (Manual)
- User adds items to a bin manually: name, description, photo, tags
- Available on all tiers

### 3. AI Item Identification (AI Scanning)
- User points camera at an item or group of items
- Claude API identifies what the item is and suggests a name, description, and tags
- Saves significant time when cataloging a full bin
- Available on Free (5 lifetime scans), Pro (50/month), and Move (150/30 days)
- Not available on Keeper tier — primary Pro upgrade hook

### 4. Public Shareable QR Codes
- Any bin can be toggled to "public" — making its contents viewable by anyone who scans the QR code, without needing a BinKeeper account
- Read-only view — public users cannot edit contents
- Viral acquisition mechanic — non-users who scan a public bin see the BinKeeper brand and can download the app
- Use cases:
  - Little Free Library — see what books are inside
  - Community seed library — what's available this season
  - Neighborhood tool share — what's in the shed
  - Church/food pantry — what's stocked this week
  - Scout troop or sports team gear box
  - Vacation home / Airbnb storage closet
  - HOA common area supplies

### 5. Household & Group Sharing
- Multiple users can share access to the same bin inventory
- Each member can view, add, and edit items
- Available on Keeper, Pro, and Move tiers
- Use cases:
  - Household members sharing a storage room
  - Sports team gear that rotates between coaches (Little League, softball, soccer)
  - Poker/game night equipment that lives at someone's house
  - Hunting or camping gear shared among a group
  - Band equipment — PA, cables, stands
  - Vacation home shared among family
  - Neighborhood tool lending
  - College roommates
  - Film/photo crew shared gear

### 6. Check-In / Check-Out
- Users can "check out" an item from a bin — logging that it has been removed and where it is going
- Check it back in when returned
- Answers the perennial group question: "Who has the thing we all use?"
- Available on all tiers

### 7. Item Search
- Full-text search across all bins and items
- Find any item instantly without remembering which bin it's in
- Available on all tiers

### 8. Seasonal Reminders
- Users can tag bins with seasons or holidays (Christmas, Halloween, Thanksgiving, Summer, etc.)
- App sends reminders when the season approaches: "Your Halloween bin is tagged for October — want to check what's inside?"
- Available on Keeper, Pro, and Move tiers
- Strong retention mechanic — brings passive users back to the app organically

### 9. Item Capacity Add-On
- Per-bin purchase of +50 items for $0.99, one-time, permanent
- Stackable — can be purchased multiple times on the same bin
- Available on Free and Keeper tiers (Pro and Move have unlimited items)
- Designed for large bins: holiday ornaments, sporting goods, workshop parts

### 10. BinKeeper Move Mode
- One-time $25 purchase, 30-day window
- Unlimited bins, unlimited items, 150 AI scans
- Purpose-built for moving — label every box before the truck arrives
- At expiry, account returns to Free tier with all data preserved read-only
- Natural upsell to Keeper or Pro occurs at expiry

## Positioning

### Primary positioning
Home and household storage organization. Label your bins, find your stuff.

### Secondary positioning
Shared physical inventory for groups. Whoever has the stuff, BinKeeper knows.

### Key differentiators
1. QR codes make physical bins instantly searchable
2. Public shareable QR — no account needed to view a bin
3. AI scanning dramatically reduces cataloging time
4. Group sharing covers use cases no other home org app addresses
5. BinKeeper Move is the only purpose-built moving inventory product in this space

---

## Viral & Growth Mechanics

- **Public QR codes** — every public bin in the wild is a branded acquisition touchpoint
- **Group sharing invites** — inviting a teammate, housemate, or group member requires them to download the app
- **Check-in/check-out notifications** — activity-based notifications keep the app visible
- **Seasonal reminders** — brings lapsed users back before every major holiday
- **BinKeeper Move word of mouth** — moving is social; people talk about their tools
