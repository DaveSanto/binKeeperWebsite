How it works

Scan it. Bin it. Find it.

01

Label your bins

BinKeeper generates a unique QR code for each bin you own. Print it, stick it on, and you're set — one scan away from the full contents list.

02

Photograph your items

Snap a photo of each item as you pack. BinKeeper uses AI to identify it automatically. Barcode on the box? Even better — we'll look it up for you.

03

Find anything, instantly

Need something? Search the app by item name, room, or bin number. Or scan a bin's QR code to see its full contents — photo and list, right there.

Features

Everything your bins deserve.

QR codes, generated and printed

Every bin gets its own QR code — generated by the app, printed by you. Stick it on the bin. Scan it later. The full contents list appears instantly on your phone, including a photo of the bin and everything inside it.

BIN-007 · Halloween Decor

scan to view contents

AI-powered item identification

Snap a photo and our AI identifies the item for you. Or scan the barcode for instant product lookup. Confirmation is always yours — no mystery labels.

Search by house, room, or bin

Can't remember which bin something is in? Search the app. Filter by location, holiday, or bin number. Every item is one tap away.

Share with your household

Everyone in the house can add items, check things out, and check them back in. One shared inventory — no more "did you see the ___?"

Check out & check in

Taking something out of the bin? Check it out and log where you're putting it. Return it later with a tap. Always know where your stuff actually is.
