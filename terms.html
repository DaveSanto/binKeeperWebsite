3. Plans and payments

Available plans

BinKeeper offers the following plans:

  • Keeper: Basic — no charge, limited to 2 bins, 10 items per bin, and 5 lifetime AI scans
  • Keeper: Everyday — $3.99/month, billed monthly, 25 bins, 40 items per bin, and 10 AI scans per month
  • Keeper: Pro — $11.99/month, billed monthly, unlimited bins and items, 50 AI scans per month
  • Keeper: Move — $25 one-time purchase, 30-day access with unlimited bins, items, and 150 AI scans
  • Item capacity add-on — $0.99 per bin for +50 additional item slots (available on Keeper: Basic and Keeper: Everyday plans, stackable)

Prices may change. We'll give you at least 30 days' notice before changing the price of a plan you're currently subscribed to.

Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period. You can cancel anytime from your account settings.

6. AI features

BinKeeper's AI Scanning feature uses Anthropic's Claude AI to identify items from photos. When you use this feature, your photo is sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. The result — a suggested item name, description, and tags — is returned to your account.

AI identification is a suggestion, not a guarantee. You always confirm or edit what the AI finds before it's saved. We're not responsible for misidentifications — always review the result before accepting it.

AI scan limits apply per plan. For Keeper: Everyday and Keeper: Pro, unused scans reset on the 1st of each month — they do not carry over. For Keeper: Move, scans are a one-time allotment for your 30-day window; any unused scans expire when the window closes. There is no next period to roll into, and the 150-scan limit is intentionally generous so you don't need to ration them during a move.
