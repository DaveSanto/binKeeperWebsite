Your bins, accounted for.

A bin inventory only works if it reflects reality. Check out / check in keeps your inventory honest — when something leaves the bin, you log where it went. When it comes back, one tap marks it returned. No more "it's in the bin" when it actually isn't.

One-tap check out

Taking something from a bin? Tap check out, log where you're putting it, and you're done. Takes less time than putting the item back improperly.

Easy return

When something comes back to the bin, mark it returned. The inventory updates immediately for everyone in the household.

Location logging

Log where a checked-out item went — "living room", "car", "lent to neighbor" — so you always know where to find it, bin or not.

Outstanding items view

See a list of everything currently out of its bin — who has it, where it is, how long it's been out. Nothing falls through the cracks.
