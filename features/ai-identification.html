Stop typing. Start snapping.

The hardest part of any organization system is entering data. BinKeeper's AI skips that entirely — take one photo, and it does the identification work for you. You just confirm what's right and skip what isn't. You're always in control; the AI just does the heavy lifting.

Photo recognition

Point your camera at the bin contents and the AI analyzes the image — identifying items, grouping similar things, and suggesting descriptive names.

Barcode scanning

Got a box with a barcode? Scan it and we'll look it up instantly — product name, brand, and description all filled in automatically.

You confirm everything

The AI suggests, you decide. Accept, edit, or skip each suggestion. Nothing gets added to your bin without your approval — no mystery labels, ever.

Gets smarter over time

The more you use BinKeeper, the better it gets at recognizing your stuff — seasonal decorations, sports gear, tools — whatever lives in your bins.
