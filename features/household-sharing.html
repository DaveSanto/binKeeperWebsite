The whole family, on the same page.

Organization falls apart when only one person knows where things are. BinKeeper's household sharing means everyone can look up, add to, and check out from the same bins — from their own phone, any time.

Unlimited members

Home and Unlimited plans support as many household members as you want. Invite everyone — kids, partners, roommates — no cap.

Simple invite

Share a link or send an invite from the app. New members join your household inventory in seconds — nothing to configure.

Shared editing

Any household member can add items, update bins, and log check-outs. The inventory stays accurate no matter who's making changes.

Activity history

See who added what, when, and who checked something out. A simple log so nothing goes missing without a trace.
